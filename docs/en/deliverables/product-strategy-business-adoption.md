# Product strategy, business model and adoption

## Value proposition

ECIWISE+ centralizes tutoring, learning, tasks, chat and academic intelligence so students receive timely support and the institution can act with better visibility.

## Target audiences

| Audience | Need | Value |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Students | Organize learning and request help | One place for study, tasks and support |
| Tutors | Manage time and follow-up | Availability, requests, schedule and history |
| Administrators | Understand risk and service usage | Metrics, predictions and user operations |
| Faculty | Connect students to support | Institutional support path |

## Business model

Best fit: institutional B2B/B2B2C.

Options:

- Annual institutional license.
- Active-user pricing.
- Premium analytics/IA modules.
- Implementation and support package.
- Academic pilot/research agreement.

## Adoption strategy

1. Pilot with selected programs and high-demand subjects.
2. Activate concrete use cases before broad feature promotion.
3. Train tutors and admins.
4. Add classroom and institutional communication.
5. Measure activation, retention, tutoring demand and satisfaction.
6. Expand into institutional processes.

## Key metrics

- Registered users.
- Profile completion.
- Weekly active users.
- Tutorship reservations.
- Attendance rate.
- Study/practice usage.
- Chat/assistant interactions.
- Students supported through risk signals.
