# Brand guide

## Brand essence

ECIWISE+ is an academic support platform that helps students find help, organize learning and receive timely guidance while giving tutors and administrators better visibility.

## Promise

Learning support in one place: tutoring, study, tasks, chat and academic intelligence connected through a single institutional experience.

## Personality

| Trait | Expression |
| --- | --- |
| Clear | Direct language and visible next steps |
| Human | Supportive, respectful and practical tone |
| Reliable | Institutional visuals and predictable behavior |
| Intelligent | Data and IA as guidance, not opaque decisions |

## Messaging

Primary message:

> Learn with support. Organize your path. Connect with the people who can help.

Supporting messages:

- ECIWISE+ centralizes academic support.
- Students get a clearer path to help.
- Tutors manage availability, requests and history.
- Administrators act with data instead of scattered signals.

## Writing rules

- Use `ECIWISE+`.
- Say "AI assistant" or "academic IA" depending on context.
- Avoid claiming perfect predictions.
- Present IA as support for human decisions.
